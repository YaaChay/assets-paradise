Gigi & Nate
Nate Gibson’s life is turned upside down after he suffers a near-fatal illness and is left a quadriplegic. Moving 
forward seems impossible until he meets his unlikely service animal, Gigi – a curious and intelligent capuchin 
monkey – who helps Nate find what he needs most: hope.
Director :Nick Hamm
Starrings:Charlie Rowe
          Zoe Margaret Colleti
          Josepine Langford
