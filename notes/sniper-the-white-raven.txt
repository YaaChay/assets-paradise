Sniper: The White Raven
Inspired by a true story: After suffering a senseless tragedy at the hand of invading soldiers in Donbas, a former 
Ukrainian physics teacher renounces his peaceful way of life and seeks revenge. Upon joining the military and earning a coveted sp
ot as a sniper, he sets his sights on an elite Russian sniper whose elimination could change the tide of the conflict.
